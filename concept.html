The Concept

 Magehem is a competitive, virtual reality (VR), tower defense game where you duel your opponent with an arsenal of spells, minions, and potions to try and destroy the opponentâ€™s tower. The game will involve rounds of combat with brief pauses in between for strategic planning. Players will use a dynamic spellcasting system that they will be able to customize, as well as potions and minions to defeat their opponent while defending their own tower.
