The Concept

 Magehem is a competitive, virtual reality (VR), tower defense game where you duel your opponent with an arsenal of spells, minions, and potions to try and destroy the opponent’s tower. The game will involve rounds of combat with brief pauses in between for strategic planning. Players will use a dynamic spellcasting system that they will be able to customize, as well as potions and minions to defeat their opponent while defending their own tower.

Game Concept

You play as a mage atop your tower and use magic, alchemy, and minions to destroy your opponents tower.

 Feature Set

The player must prevent their tower from reaching zero health while depleting the health of the opponent’s tower. The player must destroy their opponent’s tower by damaging it with spells and working to get their minions to reach the enemy's tower's base. In this game, spellcasting in done through gesture based commands. Each command builds a glyph of the spell, and by combining these glyphs, the player can create spells. The player can combine alchemical ingredients to create potions to buff themselves and their minions, as well as upgrade minions to counter their opponent’s playstyle and minion choice. 

Genre

Magehem is a tower defense magic game based in a high fantasy setting. The game is in first person and will run on the Oculus Rift. 

 Target Audience

Magehem’s goal is to be accessible to as many groups as possible. To follow through with this idea, efforts will be taken to address as many accessibility issues as possible by taking steps to mitigate the effects of motion sickness. The game will make an effort to be easy to learn to attract new players interested in VR, but also have enough depth to keep experienced VR enutsists engaged. Due industry standard suggestions VR should not be used by children younger than 13, as such the game is targeted towards ages 13+. 

 Game Flow Summary

The player will begin the game in a simple room with statues of the playable characters arrayed in front of them in a semicircle. They will reach out and grab a wand from one of these statues and the game will begin. The player will be transported to their tower were they will have to cast a spell to send off a flare, which will begin the game. During the game they will have to cast spells offensively, defensively, and supportively to survive oncoming attacks and defeat their opponent. They can find a list of possible spells inside their spellbook, casting these spells will cost mana, which will slowly regenerate. They will also be able to create potions by combining alchemical ingredients on the table in front of them and placing them in a mix in the same area. After three minutes the round will end and the player will have 30 seconds to choose which minions they want to upgrade, if any. This process will repeat once more before entering the final round. In this round players will have their mana unlocked, allowing for unlimited spell casting with no need for regeneration as the game enters sudden death. During this round, or in any round pirior, if a tower reaches 0 health, the game ends.
